About Me

I grew up in a small town in northern Ontario. I'll always be a nature lover at heart, but I also crave the opportunities bigger cities have to offer.

I’m a bilingual (English/French) Web Developer with 12 years of experience in distribution and logistics. I’ve been coding for years, but upgraded my skills in early 2018. That's when I graduated from the HackerYou College of Technology's Web Development Immersive. Now, I'm a mentor with Canada Learning Code, volunteer with ProjectWebsite.org and full-time Web Developer.

Aside from web development (which I also do in my spare time), I love books (especially Sci-Fi), plants and anything outdoors.
